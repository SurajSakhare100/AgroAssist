Solution for farmers

Solution for farmers

DeHaat Farmer App is a onestop platform that provides multiple services, including frequent crop reminders, voice calls in regional languages, crop advisories, weather reports, local mandi rates, etc. to over 1.4 million farmers in 12 major agri-based states in India. Farmers can use the App to get direct consultations from Agri-experts on chemical dosage, crop health advice, and the use of high-quality input products. Purchase and sale of input or output products can also be done through the app with convenience. Farmers can avail customised advisory services based on real-time information through the app right at their fingertips.

5 A's we offer to Empower Farmers

Access to Quality Input

Access to Quality Input

A fast home/field delivery service of quality Agri input products to the farmers. Access to quality inputs helps farmers obtain better produce with right product and dosage recommendations based on their requirements. It also helps farmers avoid the hassle of travelling long distances with convenient options like 'Payment at Delivery', 'Pickup at Nearest DeHaat Center' saving them both time and money.

Access to Affordable Insurance

Access to Affordable Insurance

A “Satellite-based parametric weather insurance” program for the farmers. Unpredictable weather poses a potential threat to the farmers' production and economic prosperity. Our affordable insurance program offers a safety net to the farmers in case of crop damage caused by natural calamities. The program is offered at a very low premium with an uncomplicated enrolment and a prompt claim settlement process.

Access to Formal Credit

Access to Formal Credit

Uncomplicated financing option for farmers to purchase various farm equipment for convenient farming practices and better produce. Formal Credit to farmers solves the credit crunch that the farming sector faces so that they can purchase the required products on credit and increase their production. Farmers are also provided with the option of paying back in easy instalments.

Access to Actionable Advisory

Access to Actionable Advisory

Renders advisory & suggestions by expert agronomists on various farming essentials. Actionable Advisory aims to impart awareness about different agricultural problems and the solutions to tackle them. This initiative will also help to increase awareness of DeHaat's quality products and services in the farming community.

E-Samadhan

Access to Profitable Markets

Access to Profitable Markets

Enables farmers to sell their produce directly to institutional buyers and improves their income. Access to Profitable Markets offers farmers the convenience to sell their harvest at the end of the cropping season at mandi rates without the hassle of transportation and involvement of middlemen.

Premium Membership Program

Premium Membership Program

A membership program for farmers to get all available solutions of DeHaat under one umbrella. Available at a budget-friendly rate with an annual payment option, Farmer Membership Program offers agricultural and financial services along with various unique benefits on a priority basis to the members.
